What CMS Europe Has Built

Years of uncompromised standards

The Court of Master Sommeliers was established in 1969. In over 50 years, it has become the most respected examining body for sommeliers in the world.

Only 293 people hold the Master Sommelier diploma. That number means something. It represents a standard that has never been compromised.

Today, CMS Europe runs courses across Europe, Oceania, Asia, and the Americas. It supports a growing network of partner schools, sponsors, and candidates at every level.

The education is world class. The reputation is unmatched.

An entire ecosystem built around that excellence — connecting candidates to careers, partners to visibility, and sponsors to the most passionate wine professionals in the world.

That is what adaptation looks like. The core stays the same. Everything around it grows.

05

The Three Pillars

Everyone becomes more valuable under the CMS Europe name.

The CMS Europe ecosystem is built on three pillars. Candidates gain a lifelong career network and community beyond the exam. Partners receive real tools and visibility to grow their schools worldwide. Sponsors connect with the sommelier profession through dedicated content and genuine access.

Each pillar has its own unique benefits, its own value, and its own role in the ecosystem. Together, under the CMS Europe name, they create something far greater than any one alone.

I

Candidates & Students

From Introductory to Master Sommelier — candidates are the heart of CMS Europe. The ecosystem extends their journey far beyond the exam:

  • Career Portal — Candidates create professional profiles, share their qualifications, and connect with employers worldwide. Wine directors, brand ambassadors, educators — the right roles find the right people.
  • CMS Europe Magazine — A quarterly publication featuring interviews with Master Sommeliers, candidate success stories, and industry insights. AI-assisted, human-led — content that keeps the community connected and growing.
  • Community & Networking — A lifelong professional network under the CMS Europe name. Not just a certificate on the wall — an active, living community that opens doors throughout an entire career.

Why this is essential for AI: The AI learns from the candidate journey — their questions, their progress, their career paths. A connected candidate network is what makes the AI genuinely knowledgeable and useful.

II

Partners

CMS Europe's partner schools in India, Australia, Hong Kong, and beyond carry the brand with pride. The ecosystem gives them real tools to grow:

  • Partner Directory — A dedicated, visible home for every CMS Europe partner school in the world. Clear, professional, easy for candidates to find.
  • Content & Marketing Support — AI-generated content in the CMS Europe voice — course announcements, social media, candidate recruitment materials — consistent quality across every market.
  • Candidate Pipeline — The ecosystem connects interested candidates directly with their nearest partner school. More visibility, more enrolments, more growth.

Why this is essential for AI: The AI speaks with one voice across every partner school in the world. Consistent content, consistent quality, consistent standards — powered by a connected partner network.

III

Sponsors

Coravin, Ruinart, Zalto, Jackson Family Wines — world class brands that deserve more than a logo. The ecosystem gives them a real presence:

  • Magazine Features — Dedicated editorial content in the CMS Europe Magazine. Brand stories, product spotlights, sommelier partnerships — authentic storytelling that reaches the most passionate wine professionals in the world.
  • AI-Powered Content — The AI creates branded content in the sponsor's voice — social posts, newsletter features, tasting notes — professional quality, produced at scale.
  • Transparent Sponsorship Programme — A clear application process, defined visibility packages, and measurable value. New brands can join the CMS Europe world with confidence.

Why this is essential for AI: Sponsors sustain the ecosystem. The AI creates genuine value for every sponsor — increasing their investment, funding the growth of the entire system.

06

Why We Start Slow

The AI is the wine. The data is the grape. First, we tend the vineyard.

Everyone is talking about AI. But very few people are building it the right way.

The most common mistake is to rush. To grab a tool, install it, and call it done. That is not AI. That is a shortcut.

Shortcuts in AI produce the wrong results — answers that sound confident but are wrong, content that feels generic, systems that do not understand who you are or what you stand for.

Building AI the right way starts with one thing: organized, high quality data.

Before any AI can speak in the voice of CMS Europe, it needs to know CMS Europe deeply. Its history. Its standards. Its tone of voice.

Its candidates, its partners, its sponsors. The way a Master Sommelier thinks. The way CMS Europe communicates. The values that have never changed in 50 years.

This is why Phase 1 is not about technology. It is about knowledge.

We sit together. We listen. We document. We organize. We collect every piece of information that makes CMS Europe what it is — and we structure it in a way that will one day train the most knowledgeable sommelier AI in the world.

The AI is the wine. The data is the grape. And right now, we need to tend the vineyard.

No great wine was ever made from poor fruit.

How We Get There — Step by Step

Listen first. Build second. Scale together.

Phase 1 — Months 1–3 — 2026

The Discovery Plan

Every great project begins with listening. Phase 1 is a three month discovery process. No technology yet. No AI yet. Just deep, focused work to understand everything CMS Europe is today.

Week by week, we meet. We review the website, the LinkedIn presence, the course materials, the partner relationships, the sponsor agreements. We document the tone of voice, the visual identity, the writing style, the values.

Three clear deliverables by the end of three months:

1

Brand & Voice Document

The DNA of CMS Europe, written down and structured for the first time.

2

Full Content & Data Audit

Everything that exists today, organized and ready to feed the future AI.

3

Phase 2 Roadmap

Exactly what we build, in what order, and why.

Phase 2 — Months 4–12 — 2026

The Build

With the foundation in place, we start building. The first thing we build is the CMS Europe AI — trained on the voice, the standards, the knowledge, the history. Not a generic chatbot. An AI that thinks and speaks like CMS Europe.

Alongside, we launch the CMS Europe magazine — quarterly, AI-assisted but human-led. We rebuild the LinkedIn and social media presence. We build the partner directory. We create a transparent sponsor system.

By the end of Phase 2, CMS Europe has a voice, a presence, a community, and an AI that knows its world better than any tool ever has.

Phase 3 — 2027–2030

Scale — The Full Ecosystem

The centerpiece of Phase 3 is the CMS Europe Career Portal — a full professional platform. Candidates register their profile and share their journey. Employers post opportunities. The AI matches the right person to the right role.

Not just restaurants. Wine directors for cruise lines. Brand ambassadors for wineries. Key account managers. Educators. Consultants. The future of the sommelier profession.

And there is one more role just beginning to emerge — the AI-literate sommelier. Wineries and hospitality groups need people who understand both wine and AI. CMS Europe prepares its candidates for exactly that future.

SOMM DIGI does not build this and walk away. We build it together and transfer knowledge every step of the way. By 2030, CMS Europe will have its own AI capability. SOMM DIGI will always be the architect. But the house will belong to CMS Europe.

The sommelier profession is evolving.
Candidates of 2030 will expect more than a certificate.

The institutions that adapt early will lead for the next 50 years. The ecosystem makes sure CMS Europe is ready.
