02

What You've Built — And What's Changing

50 years of excellence. A world that is catching up.

1969

Year established

293

Master Sommeliers worldwide

50+

Years of uncompromised standards

The Court of Master Sommeliers was established in 1969. In over 50 years, it has become the most respected examining body for sommeliers in the world.

Only 293 people hold the Master Sommelier diploma. That number means something. It represents a standard that has never been compromised.

Today, CMS Europe runs courses across Europe, Oceania, Asia, and the Americas. It supports a growing network of partner schools, sponsors, and candidates at every level.

But the world around it is changing fast.

Candidates want more than a certification. They want a community, a career, a voice.

Partner schools carry the CMS brand with pride but need more support to grow. Sponsors want real visibility, not just a logo on a page.

The sommelier profession itself is evolving — beyond the restaurant floor, into new industries, new roles, new opportunities.

CMS Europe has everything it needs to lead this new world. The reputation. The network. The standards. What it needs now is the right partner to help it adapt.

03

The Next 50 Years

Not a technology project. An adaptation.

The vision is simple. CMS Europe becomes the leading sommelier ecosystem in the world.

Not just an exam body. A full, living community — where candidates grow, partners thrive, sponsors connect, and the sommelier profession finds its future.

We call this the Road to 2030. It is not a technology project. It is an adaptation.

A natural evolution of everything CMS Europe has already built — extended into the digital world, supported by AI, and guided by the same values that have defined the Court for over half a century.

At the center is one powerful idea: everything we build feeds everything else.

The content feeds the AI. The AI powers the magazine. The magazine builds the community. The community attracts the sponsors. The sponsors fund the growth. And the growth brings more candidates, more partners, more sommeliers into the world of CMS Europe.

Adaptation is not the end of tradition. It is how tradition survives.

04

Who Benefits

Candidates. Partners. Sponsors. Everyone grows.

The CMS Europe ecosystem serves three distinct groups. Each one has a different need. Each one represents a different opportunity. And each one is essential to making the whole system work.

Candidates

From Introductory to Master Sommelier — candidates are the heart of CMS Europe. The ecosystem transforms them from certificate holders into lifelong members. A job portal. A magazine. A community built for them.

Partners

Partner schools in India, Australia, Hong Kong and beyond carry the brand with pride. The ecosystem gives them a home — a dedicated directory, content support, and real visibility that helps them grow.

Sponsors

Coravin, Ruinart, Zalto — world class brands that deserve more than a logo. The ecosystem gives them a real voice, magazine features, AI-powered content, and genuine access to the sommelier community.

05

Why We Start Slow

The AI is the wine. The data is the grape. First, we tend the vineyard.

Everyone is talking about AI. But very few people are building it the right way.

The most common mistake is to rush. To grab a tool, install it, and call it done. That is not AI. That is a shortcut.

Shortcuts in AI produce the wrong results — answers that sound confident but are wrong, content that feels generic, systems that do not understand who you are or what you stand for.

Building AI the right way starts with one thing: organized, high quality data.

Before any AI can speak in the voice of CMS Europe, it needs to know CMS Europe deeply. Its history. Its standards. Its tone of voice.

Its candidates, its partners, its sponsors. The way a Master Sommelier thinks. The way CMS Europe communicates. The values that have never changed in 50 years.

This is why Phase 1 is not about technology. It is about knowledge.

We sit together. We listen. We document. We organize. We collect every piece of information that makes CMS Europe what it is — and we structure it in a way that will one day train the most knowledgeable sommelier AI in the world.

The AI is the wine. The data is the grape. And right now, we need to tend the vineyard.

No great wine was ever made from poor fruit.

How We Get There — Step by Step

Listen first. Build second. Scale together.

Phase 1 — Months 1–3 — 2026

The Discovery Plan

Every great project begins with listening. Phase 1 is a three month discovery process. No technology yet. No AI yet. Week by week, we meet. We review the website, the LinkedIn presence, the course materials, the partner relationships, the sponsor agreements. We document the tone of voice, the visual identity, the writing style, the values.

Three clear deliverables by the end of three months:

1

Brand & Voice Document

The DNA of CMS Europe, written down and structured for the first time.

2

Full Content & Data Audit

Everything that exists today, organized and ready to feed the future AI.

3

Phase 2 Roadmap

Exactly what we build, in what order, and why.

Phase 2 — Months 4–12 — 2026

The Build

With the foundation in place, we start building. The first thing we build is the CMS Europe AI — trained on the voice, the standards, the knowledge, the history. Not a generic chatbot. An AI that thinks and speaks like CMS Europe. It knows the curriculum. It knows the candidates. It is the brain of the ecosystem.

Alongside, we launch the CMS Europe magazine — quarterly, AI-assisted but human-led. We rebuild the LinkedIn and social media presence. We build the partner directory. We create a transparent sponsor system. By the end of Phase 2, CMS Europe has a voice, a presence, a community, and an AI that knows its world better than any tool ever has.

Phase 3 — 2027–2030

Scale

Phase 3 is where the ecosystem reaches its full potential. The centerpiece is the CMS Europe Career Portal — a full professional platform. Candidates register. Employers post. The AI matches the right person to the right role. Not just restaurants. Wine directors for cruise lines. Brand ambassadors for wineries. Key account managers. Educators. Consultants. The future of the sommelier profession.

And there is one more role just beginning to emerge — the AI-literate sommelier. Wineries, wine brands, and hospitality groups need people who understand both wine and AI. Through this ecosystem, CMS Europe prepares its candidates for exactly that future. The CMS Europe graduate of 2030 will be the most complete wine professional in the world.

SOMM DIGI does not build this and walk away. We build it together and transfer knowledge every step of the way. By 2030, CMS Europe will have its own AI capability. SOMM DIGI will always be the architect. But the house will belong to CMS Europe.
