Step 01

The Excellence

50 years of uncompromised standards. The strongest foundation in the world.

1969

Year established

293

50+

Years of uncompromised standards

The Court of Master Sommeliers was established in 1969. In over 50 years, it has become the most respected examining body for sommeliers in the world.

Only 293 people hold the Master Sommelier diploma. That number means something. It represents a standard that has never been compromised.

Today, CMS Europe runs courses across Europe, Oceania, Asia, and the Americas. It supports a growing network of partner schools, sponsors, and candidates at every level.

The education is world class. The reputation is unmatched. The core values and traditions are the foundation of everything that follows.

Step 02

The World Is Changing

The sommelier profession is evolving. The institutions that adapt first will lead.

AI is becoming part of every industry. The sommelier world is no exception.

Candidates of 2030 will expect more than a certificate. They will expect a community, a career network, and tools that prepare them for a world where AI is part of daily professional life.

Partner schools need consistent content, real visibility, and modern tools to grow. Sponsors want genuine access to the community, not just a logo on a page.

The sommelier of the future will not just know wine. The sommelier of the future will know how to work with AI — building wine programmes, creating content, training teams, and connecting with a new generation of wine professionals.

The institutions that adapt early will lead for the next 50 years. The institutions that wait will follow.

The Goal

CMS Europe becomes the world's first AI-adapted sommelier organization — without losing a single tradition.

Step 03

The Goal

Not change. Adaptation. The core stays. Everything around it evolves.

CMS Europe's education, examinations, and standards remain exactly as they are. Nothing changes about what makes the Court great.

The goal is to build an ecosystem around that excellence — connecting candidates to careers, partners to visibility, sponsors to the community — all powered by AI that deeply understands CMS Europe.

This is not a technology project. This is an adaptation. Like great wine, it takes time. It is an ageing process — built carefully, year by year.

The traditions stay. The values stay. The standards stay. The ecosystem grows around them.

Step 04

The Ecosystem

AI needs a foundation. That foundation is the ecosystem.

AI without a foundation produces poor results. Shortcuts lead to generic answers, wrong information, and content that does not represent CMS Europe.

To build AI that truly speaks in the voice of the Court, an entire ecosystem must be built first — organising candidates, partners, and sponsors into one connected system.

CMS Europe is the vine — strong, rooted, proven. The ecosystem is the soil, the climate, the cellar, the ageing process. Everything that allows the vine to produce its finest wine.

At the centre is one powerful idea: everything that is built feeds everything else. The content feeds the AI. The AI powers the magazine. The magazine builds the community. The community attracts the sponsors. The sponsors fund the growth. And the growth brings more candidates, more partners, more sommeliers into the world of CMS Europe.

Adaptation is not the end of tradition. It is how tradition survives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers. No ambiguity.

Does the ecosystem change how CMS Europe operates? +

No. CMS Europe's education, examinations, and standards remain exactly as they are. The ecosystem is built entirely around them — not inside them. The tradition stays untouched. The ecosystem extends its reach into the new world.

Does SOMM DIGI have any involvement with the CMS Europe trademark or brand identity? +

No. The CMS Europe name, brand, and identity belong entirely to CMS Europe. SOMM DIGI builds the ecosystem to serve and protect that identity — never to alter it. Every piece of content, every AI output, every design element follows the standards and voice of CMS Europe.

What does "adaptation" mean in this context? +

The world is changing. AI is becoming part of every industry. Adaptation means making CMS Europe ready for this new world — without changing anything about what makes it great. The same excellence, the same standards, the same tradition — now extended into a digital ecosystem that connects candidates, partners, and sponsors in ways that were not possible before.

Why an ecosystem and not just AI? +

AI without a foundation produces poor results. The ecosystem organises candidates, partners, and sponsors into a connected system — and that connected system is what the AI needs to work properly. The ecosystem is the vineyard. The AI is the wine. One cannot exist without the other.

Who owns everything that is built? +

CMS Europe. Every deliverable, every piece of content, every system, every AI model. SOMM DIGI is the architect. The house belongs to CMS Europe. By 2030, CMS Europe will have its own AI capability and full ownership of the entire ecosystem.

What happens after the first six months? +

SOMM DIGI and CMS Europe review the progress together and align on the next phase. All work delivered during the first six months belongs to CMS Europe regardless of any future decisions. There is no lock-in. The partnership continues only if both sides see the value.
